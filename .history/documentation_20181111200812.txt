Every budding musician (and seasoned ones too) should know the circle of fifths.
Use this skill to test your knowledge of the circle in intervals of fourths or fifths.

Each test consists of x multiple choice questions.
You simply need to reply to each question with the number of the correct answer.

If you are new to the circle of fifths you can print this diagram to assist you until you are able to do the tests from memory.
https://www.techros.info/circle-of-fifths-diagram

The app card will show the questions and answers for each test taken.
